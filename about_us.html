About Us

we start since 1990

Chandak Chambers is a multi-functional, inter-disciplinary law firm, which primarily has two streams, one being Jus-Juris (P.C.Chandak & Associates) which practises in the area of litigation & non-litigation issues, and another being Personnel Solutions (Prakash Bandhu) which renders industrial consultancy.

Jus Juris being the legal wing of Chandak Chambers, with its chief office in the Sanskardhani of Madhya Pradesh wherein the principal seat of High Court is situated, has associates spread over the entire State of Madhya Pradesh as well as different parts of India. It provides litigation & non-litigation advisory services in Arbitration, Civil-disputes, Contractual issues, Company law, Labour & Industrial laws, Service laws to name a few.

Personnel Solutions is fondly remembered by its clients who are spread over all over India as ‘Prakash Bandhu: Industrial Consultants’, and its advisory services covers the hiatus between the compliances under labour laws & business interests. It primarily provides varied services in provident fund (PF), employees state insurance (ESI), Registration under Factories & Shop acts, and facilitates determination or computation of minimum wages, bonus, gratuity, etc.
