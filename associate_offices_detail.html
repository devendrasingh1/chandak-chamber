Mr. Abhishek nath tripathi

Corporate Advisor

Abhishek Tripathi [D/400/2005], the founder of Abhishek Tripathi & Associates has several years of professional experience in corporate transactional and litigation lawyering in India. He graduated from the National Law School of India University at Bangalore, one of India's premier law schools and worked with Luthra & Luthra Law Offices at New Delhi for several years. He was the Senior Associate in the Mergers & Acquisitions, Joint Ventures and Private Equity Team at Luthra & Luthra.

He has been practicing a wide range of laws, such as foreign exchange laws, companies law, securities laws, insurance laws and telecom laws. He routinely interacts with governmental and regulatory authorities in India, such as Reserve Bank of India, Securities and Exchange Bank of India (SEBI), Foreign Investment Promotion Board (FIPB), Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion (DIPP), Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority (IRDA), Department of Telecommunication (DoT) and Telecom Regulatory Authority of India on several matters.

Abhishek is also a visiting faculty for insurance laws and corporate laws at the National Law School of India University, Bangalore and the Amity Law School at New Delhi. He is also associated with the International Academy of Insurance & Financial Management in New Delhi, India, a leading organization involved in training insurance professionals, as an expert on insurance laws.
